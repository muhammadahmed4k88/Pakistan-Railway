Allama Iqbal Express

The Allama Iqbal Express is one of the oldest express train
of Pakistan, beginning in 1940 as the Shaheen Express from
Karachi to Sialkot via Faisalabad. its timing is
3:30pm from Karachi

Karakoram Express

The karakoram Express is the trains of Pakistan, beginning in
2002 as the Express from Karachi to Lahore via Faisalabad.
its timing is 3:00pm from Karachi
